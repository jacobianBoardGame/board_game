Chip Avalon Full Rules

This page stores the formal rule text for long-term maintenance on GitHub Pages, with room for revisions and variants.

基础单位

i = 1, j = 5, z = 2

i 为基础计分单位；j 为主局系数；z 为入场费系数。

Base Units

i = 1, j = 5, z = 2

i is the base scoring unit; j is the main-game multiplier; z is the entry-fee multiplier.

1. 主局结算

  • 胜利阵营每人 + i × j
  • 失败阵营每人 - i × j

1. Main Settlement

  • Each member of the winning camp receives + i × j
  • Each member of the losing camp receives - i × j

2. 特殊结算

  • 狼人炸车获胜：狼人每人再 +i，好人每人再 -i
  • 刺客刺中梅林获胜：狼人每人再 +i，好人每人再 -i，刺客再 +i，梅林再 -i
  • 好人三次平安发车且最终获胜：好人每人再 +i，狼人每人再 -i
  • 若最终狼人靠炸车获胜，且全局有两狼或三狼参与过炸车：
  • 两狼参与：狼人每人再 +i，好人每人再 -i
  • 三狼参与：狼人每人再 +2i，好人每人再 -2i

2. Special Settlement

  • Evil wins by mission sabotage: each evil player gains +i, each good player loses -i
  • Assassin correctly kills Merlin: each evil player gains +i, each good player loses -i; the Assassin gains an additional +i, Merlin loses an additional -i
  • Good achieves three clean missions and wins the game: each good player gains +i, each evil player loses -i
  • If evil wins through sabotage and two or three evil players have sabotaged missions during the game:
  • Two evil participants: each evil player gains +i, each good player loses -i
  • Three evil participants: each evil player gains +2i, each good player loses -2i

3. 发车盘（小盘口）

  • 每轮任务前可开盘：“本轮发车是否成功”
  • 需达成赔付平衡后方可成立
  • 押成功：中 +i，错 -i
  • 押失败：中 +i，错 -i

3. Mission Bet (Side Bet)

  • Before each mission, a side market may be opened: “Will this mission succeed?”
  • The market must be payout-balanced before it becomes valid
  • Bet on success: correct +i, wrong -i
  • Bet on failure: correct +i, wrong -i

4. 身份盘（小盘口）

  • 全局最多开 2 次，建议在第 2、4 发车轮
  • 猜某人红蓝：中 +i，错 -i
  • 猜某人具体身份：中 +i，错 -i

4. Identity Bet (Side Bet)

  • At most 2 identity markets may be opened per game, suggested on mission rounds 2 and 4
  • Guess a player’s camp (good/evil): correct +i, wrong -i
  • Guess a player’s exact role: correct +i, wrong -i

5. 限制

  • 每人每盘最多下注一次
  • 强制下注
  • 身份盘中，同一玩家同类盘口不可重复开

5. Restrictions

  • Each player may place at most one bet in each market
  • Betting is mandatory
  • In identity markets, the same type of market cannot be opened repeatedly for the same player

6. 最终结算

  • 每局开始，玩家支付入场费 z × i 作为公共池
  • 小赌局单独平衡结算
  • 最终主局只算原始分
  • 正分的人收钱，负分的人付钱
  • 小数部分由公共奖池支付
  • 胜利方瓜分奖池

6. Final Settlement

  • At the start of each game, every player pays an entry fee of z × i into the public pool
  • Side bets are settled separately in a balanced manner
  • The final main-game settlement uses only the original main-game score
  • Players with positive scores receive money; players with negative scores pay money
  • Decimal remainders are covered by the public pool
  • The winning side shares the prize pool

7. 扩展空间

  • 可加入 3 / 4 / 5 轮不同倍率的快胜奖励
  • 可加入 FAQ、示例局和边界情况说明
  • 可加入更多身份、不同人数模式与变体盘口

7. Extension Space

  • Faster-win bonuses for 3 / 4 / 5 round outcomes can be added
  • FAQ, sample games, and edge-case notes can be added later
  • More roles, player-count modes, and side-market variants can be supported
