錦標賽榜單

一場「遊戲（game）」= 一次坐下來的多局對局。每位玩家在同一場遊戲裡可能既當過紅方也當過藍方，最終只有一個整場的 finalScore。

數據來源：assets/data/tournament.json。新增一場遊戲 = 在 games[] 裡 push 一條記錄。

Tournament Leaderboard

One game is one sitting with multiple rounds. A player can play as red and as blue within the same game; only one finalScore is settled at the end.

Data source: assets/data/tournament.json. Add a game by appending to games[].
